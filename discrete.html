Induction is a very powerful proof technique that you will use a lot in discrete math. Here's the general outline of how to use it

Base Case

This is usually P(0) or P(1) (depends on what would be considered the starting value for your statement.). Proving this should be trivial.

Inductive Hypothesis

This is when you say that your statement holds for any value k, so assume P(k) is true.

Inductive Step

This is when you prove that your statement holds for P(k+1). By proving this, you can confirm that your statement always holds.
